Resources for the Ostreobium SAG 6.99 nuclear genome

Annotated scaffolds: Sequences in fasta format (link) with gene models in gff3 format (link). For those preferring Genbank entries, those are here

Raw DNA reads: Nanopore R9.5 (ERR4683117, ERR4683118), Illumina HiSeq (ERR4332232, ERR4332233)

Protein-coding sequences: CDS sequences in fasta (link), as amino-acid sequences (link) and table of Uniprot and KEGG functional annotations (link)

RNAseq: Raw reads from single pooled library from multiple growth conditions (link)

Organelles: Mitochondrion (link) and chloroplast (link)

Sources: Nuclear genome (10.1016/j.cub.2021.01.018), mitochondrion & RNAseq (10.7717/peerj.8273), chloroplast (10.1093/gbe/evw206)
